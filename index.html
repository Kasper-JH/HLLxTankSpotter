How To Use HLLxTankSpotter

This tool is designed to help you quickly identify and call out tanks in Hell Let Loose based on their visual characteristics.

Type: Describes whether the tank is an SPA, Recon, Light, Medium or Heavy.

Callsign: Commonly used name for the tank, which is often used in the leadership voicechat.

Details: Highlights specific visual features to help you distinguish between different tank models.

Faction

Choose the faction of your enemy (Germany, United States, Soviet Union, or British).

Variation

Pick the specific variation of tanks appearance you are encountering (e.g. Winter camo).

Map Selector

Not sure which Faction or Variation to pick?

Use the Map Selector to find out which factions and variations are present on the map you are playing on.
